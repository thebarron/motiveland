Niall

photography + development + ecommerce

Niall is relentlessly focused on bringing watch manufacturing back to the United States, offering an ever-evolving luxury timepiece. Niall came to Motive with a well established brand, seeking photography and development for their online experience.

At the forefront of the Niall brand is large format photography, offering a detailed look at the handcrafted nature of a luxury good. The implementation of this photography and story was important in creating a digital manifestation of what Niall is doing to revitalize US manufacturing.
