Envy

design + development + ecommerce + photography

Envy Gaming is boutique store focused on bringing beauty and professionalism to the personal gaming experience. The brand flourishes as a provider of hand-tailored video game products for the niche of professional gamers. As part of the founding team, Motive was responsive for building out the entire ecommerce experience, including photography and development, all the way to optimizing supply chain operations.

The brand was built on giving gamers customizable products that were only limited by their own imagination. Our challenge was to push an ecommerce platform to have all the capabilities needed for customization, while keeping the experience clean and easy.
