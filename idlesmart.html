Idle Smart

user interface + mobile design

Idle Smart is an automated engine start, stop solution that reduces overnight idle time, provides year-round cabin comfort and maintains fleet uptime by keeping batteries charged and engines warm

The Idle Smart team came to Motive with a refreshed product idea, moving their system to a 7inch Android tablet. Our team executed the design and experience for the application, focusting on a simple dashboard for untrained users to easily navigate.
